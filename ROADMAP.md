# ProcureGrid Roadmap

## Sprint 0: Product and Architecture

Define the MVP, user journeys, tenant model, wireframes, and security boundaries.

## Sprint 1: Authentication and Company Foundation

Deliver email and phone authentication, OTP verification, company creation, first-member roles,
protected routing, and tenant-aware Supabase policies.

## Sprint 2: Buyer and Supplier Workspaces

Harden authentication and onboarding, complete role-aware buyer and supplier shells, improve
localization, and stabilize profile and company settings.

## Sprint 3: Supplier Verification

Add supplier profiles, legal documents, verification states, and platform-admin review.

## Sprint 4: RFQ Workflow

Enable buyer RFQ creation, admin review, approved supplier assignment, and supplier RFQ inboxes.

## Sprint 5: Quotations and Selection

Support supplier quotation submission, buyer comparison, shortlisting, and selection with complete
tenant authorization and audit history.
